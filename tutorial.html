How To Play

Welcome to -our game-. Today´s mission is to be able to get to our home planet through cybersecurity, however we are going to be receiving many attacks, are you ready for this challenge?

1. Click in start a new game, or log in to continue past games

2. Follow each tab´s instructions and think wisely before making a decision

3. Everytime you make a correct decision, you will move forward, closer to our final goal, but if you make a mistake the game will be over and you will have to start all over again.

4. We work hard to create an accesible game for everyone and really appreciate feedback. Once you finish exploring our game, it would be amazing if you could finish one last task by flling this feedback survey
